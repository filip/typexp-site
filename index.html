How it works

Up and running in minutes.

Four steps and you're expanding text everywhere on your Mac.

01

Install & permit

Download and open Typexp, then grant Accessibility permission in System Settings. Typexp will guide you through it.

02

Create a snippet

Click the menu bar icon, open Settings, and add a snippet. A short trigger like sigwork for your email sign-off, or rfc for a React component stub.

03

Type & expand

In any app, type your trigger and press Space or Return. Watch it expand instantly into the full text you defined.

04

Add variables

Wrap words in {{curly braces}} to turn them into fill-in fields. Perfect for templates, emails, and code.
